Our Perfumes

J’ADORE EAU DE TOILETTE ROLLER-PEARL

The travel-friendly J'adore eau de toilette bottle is topped with a delicate pearl for discreet and sensorial perfuming. The soft fragrance bead rolls voluptuously over the skin, depositing the floral and citrusy notes that characterize the fragrance, composed around a heart note of neroli from Vallauris. Anywhere, at any time, this bottle, as beautiful as a gem, releases the trail of J'adore Eau de Toilette with precision on pulse points – the neck, the wrists or the décolleté. The practical roller-pearl slips into any bag for easy travel.

JOY BY DIOR

A pure concentration of joy in a bottle. JOY by Dior Eau de Parfum is a bright smile. A juicy, vibrant top note gives way to an enveloping white heart of flowers and musks, like a soft caress.
